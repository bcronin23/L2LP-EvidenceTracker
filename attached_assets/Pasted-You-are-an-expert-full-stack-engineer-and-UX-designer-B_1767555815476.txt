1) Portfolio approach: Over the three years of junior cycle, students assemble evidence of learning in a portfolio which can be hard copy and/or electronic. Evidence examples include written work, project/art work, photos, video recordings of participation/achievement, and recordings of musical work. (Implement as a digital portfolio per student.)
